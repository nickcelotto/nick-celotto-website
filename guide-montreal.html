Old Montreal
July 2026

The historic heart of the city — cobblestone streets, 17th century architecture, the St. Lawrence River waterfront. The most photogenic part of Montreal.

⛪ Notre-Dame Basilica 🌊 Old Port waterfront 📸 Cobblestone streets
Plateau-Mont-Royal
July 2026

The creative neighborhood — colorful Victorian rowhouses, indie cafés, street art, and the best food in the city. The most lived-in and interesting part of Montreal.

🎨 Street art 🍽️ Food scene ☕ Indie cafés 🏘️ Victorian rowhouses
Mont Royal
July 2026

The mountain park in the middle of the city designed by Frederick Law Olmsted (same as Central Park). Best views of the Montreal skyline from the Kondiaronk Belvedere lookout.

🌲 Mountain park 🌆 City skyline views 🥾 Hiking trails
