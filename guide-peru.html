Day by day

The plan as it stands. Notes and photos coming after the trip.

Day 0 Saturday, September 12 New York → Lima

Fly out of JFK at 11:59pm on LATAM. Overnight flight to Lima landing at 6:55am the next morning.

Day 1 Sunday, September 13 Lima

Land in Lima at 6:55am. First day in Peru — check in, recover, and explore. Lima gets dismissed as a layover city but it has one of the best food scenes in South America. Paragliding in the afternoon off the cliffs in Miraflores over the Pacific.

Day 2 Monday, September 14 Lima → Huacachina

Breakfast and a morning in Lima, then bus to Huacachina at 11am — arriving around 3pm. Huacachina is a tiny oasis village surrounded by enormous sand dunes. Dune buggy and sandboarding in the afternoon, sunset from the top of the dunes, late dinner at the oasis.

Day 3 Tuesday, September 15 Huacachina → Lima

Morning in the dunes, then bus back to Lima at 10am arriving around 2pm. Last evening in Lima — the food scene is worth spending time in before heading to the highlands.

Day 4 Wednesday, September 16 Lima → Cusco → Ollantaytambo

Fly Lima to Cusco at noon — going from sea level to 3,400m in 90 minutes. Private car from Cusco down into the Sacred Valley to Ollantaytambo, which sits lower and is an easier place to acclimatize. Late dinner and sleep.

Day 5 Thursday, September 17 Ollantaytambo → Aguas Calientes

Morning exploring Ollantaytambo — the Plaza de Armas and the Pinkuylluna ruins on the hillside above the town. Train to Aguas Calientes at 2pm, the small town at the base of Machu Picchu. Dinner and early night before the morning entrance tomorrow.

Day 6 Friday, September 18 Machu Picchu → Cusco

Early entrance to Machu Picchu at 8am — the citadel sits at 2,430m in the cloud forest between two mountain peaks. Spend the morning before the crowds build. Train back to Cusco at 2:30pm, arriving at 6:20pm. Dinner and explore Cusco at night.

Day 7 Saturday, September 19 Cusco → Lima

Morning exploring Cusco — the Plaza de Armas and the city at altitude before the flight back to Lima. One last night in Peru.

Day 8 Sunday, September 20 Lima → New York

Chill morning in Lima, pack up. Fly out at noon, land in New York at 9:05pm.
