Travel

Documenting the places I go, the photos I take, and the things worth remembering.

" Traveling is how I get out of my own head
and into the world.

I started traveling solo in 2026 and haven't stopped planning since. There's something about navigating a new place on your own — figuring out transit, finding the good spots, getting genuinely lost — that changes how you think. I come back from every trip seeing things differently.

I document everything. Day by day itineraries, real costs, honest impressions. Not as a travel blogger, just because I like having the record and other people find it useful. Every journal entry here is a place I actually went, not somewhere I researched from a desk.
